# Road Safety Intelligence & Casualty Analytics Dashboard

> **Interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using Power BI to analyze road accident trends, casualty distribution, and key safety indicators through executive-level KPI reporting and interactive visual analytics.**

---

## Project Overview

Road safety organizations and decision-makers require timely, data-driven insights to understand accident patterns, evaluate casualty trends, and identify high-risk conditions. Static reports often make it difficult to compare historical performance, explore contributing factors, and support informed planning.

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard that consolidates accident records into a dynamic analytical solution. By integrating KPI monitoring, Year-over-Year (YoY) analysis, trend visualization, and multidimensional reporting, the dashboard enables efficient exploration of road safety performance across temporal, environmental, geographic, and operational dimensions.

---

## Business Objective

Develop an executive-level Business Intelligence dashboard capable of:

- Monitoring road accident and casualty performance
- Comparing current and previous year trends
- Identifying high-risk vehicle categories and road types
- Evaluating accident distribution across environmental conditions
- Supporting data-driven road safety assessment through interactive reporting

# Dashboard Features

### Executive KPI Cards

Provides a high-level overview of casualty and accident statistics with comparative performance against the previous year.

### Monthly Trend Analysis

Visualizes accident and casualty patterns across months to identify seasonal variations and long-term trends.

### Vehicle Category Analysis

Evaluates casualty distribution across different vehicle categories, helping identify major contributors to road accidents.

### Road Type Analysis

Compares accident frequency across different road classifications to highlight high-risk infrastructure segments.

### Geographic Distribution

Maps accident locations to provide regional visibility and support location-based safety assessment.

### Urban vs Rural Comparison

Analyzes accident occurrence based on settlement type to understand differences in risk exposure.

### Environmental Condition Analysis

Examines the relationship between accident occurrence and:

- Weather Conditions
- Road Surface Conditions
- Lighting Conditions (Day/Night)

### Interactive Filtering

Supports dynamic exploration using slicers for:

- Accident Year
- Road Surface
- Weather
- Location
- Road Type
- Vehicle Category
